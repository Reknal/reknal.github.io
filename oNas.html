    Jarosław "pashaBiceps" Jarząbkowski

He currently plays for Virtus.pro.

    Jakub "kuben" Gurczyński

    Jakub "kuben" Gurczyński (born July 12, 1988) is a retired Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player and former Counter-Strike 1.6 player. Best known for his time in the Golden Five lineup, it was announced in February 2015 that he would emerge from retirement to work as a Coach for his former teammates in Virtus.pro.
