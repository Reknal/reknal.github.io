G2 esports

"G2 Esports (also called as G2.Kinguin) is a Spanish professional eSports organization located in Madrid, founded in November 2013 by Carlos "ocelote" Rodriguez. The organization also hosts squads in League of Legends, Call of Duty, and Heroes of the Storm. The organization renamed from "Gamers2" to G2 Esports as a part of their 2015 rebranding project."  http://wiki.teamliquid.net/counterstrike/G2_Esports

SK Gaming

"SK Gaming is a German eSports organization. Founded in 1997 under the name "Schroet Kommando" in Oberhausen, Germany, it has since then grown into one of the largest and most well-known eSports organizations in the world, and they are primarily known for their hugely successful Counter-Strike team."  http://wiki.teamliquid.net/counterstrike/SK_Gaming
