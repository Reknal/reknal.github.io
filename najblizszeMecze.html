    G2 esports

    "G2 Esports (also called as G2.Kinguin) is a Spanish professional eSports organization located in Madrid, founded in November 2013 by Carlos "ocelote" Rodriguez.     SK Gaming

    "SK Gaming is a German eSports organization. Founded in 1997 under the name "Schroet Kommando" in Oberhausen, Germany, it has since then grown into one of the largest and most well-known eSports organizations in the world, and they are primarily known for their hugely successful Counter-Strike team."  http://wiki.teamliquid.net/counterstrike/SK_Gaming
